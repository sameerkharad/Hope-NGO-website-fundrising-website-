Who are we?

At HOPE, we are dedicated to bringing hope and support to underprivileged children by providing essential items like clothes, footwear, and school accessories. Through our initiative Asha, we also empower women by promoting handmade bags and pouches, helping them achieve financial independence. Our platform enables generous donors to contribute through monetary donations, self-courier options, and pickup/drop services, ensuring every child gets the care they deserve. Together, we strive to make a meaningful impact, one donation at a time
